HO HSINHUNG

Born in 1994 in Chiayi, Taiwan. Completed the Advanced Course at Asagaya College of Art and Design in 2023. Entered the Department of Oil Painting at Musashino Art University the same year, and graduated in 2025. Currently enrolled in the Graduate School of Art and Design, Oil Painting Course at Musashino Art University.
