HO HSINHUNG | He Xinhong

Born in 1994 in Chiayi, Taiwan. Moved to Japan in 2017. Completed the Advanced Course at Asagaya College of Art and Design in 2023. Entered Musashino Art University, Department of Oil Painting, and graduated in 2025. Currently enrolled in the Graduate School of Art and Design at Musashino Art University.

HO HSINHUNG | 何信宏
